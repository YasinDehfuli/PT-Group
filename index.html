Petrochemical Plant Components

Petrochemical plants convert natural resources such as crude oil and natural gas liquids into products for a wide range of applications. These plants produce many important building blocks for industrial processes, including ethylene, propylene, butadiene, and aromatics. Linde is a world-leading provider of steam cracking technology for gaseous and liquid hydrocarbons and for separation of cracked gas products into important raw materials for downstream petrochemical processes. Petrochemical plants have increased in size and complexity, and we have the technology and engineering, procurement and construction (EPC) expertise to successfully build these complex facilities – no matter how big.
